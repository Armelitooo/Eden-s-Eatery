AboutUs

Why Choose us?

“Eat with you, Eat with me, Eat with everyone, Eat in Eden’s Eatery.” Eden’s Eatery started in 2017 and is located in Central Plaza Terminal, Daet, Camarines Norte . Eden Rosales is the owner and also the person who cooks the different dishes. Our Eatery is open every Tuesday to Sunday from 7 am to 7 pm for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. In Eden’s Eatery, you can choose delicious home cooked foods and very affordable to anyone. Our aim is to satisfy our customers with our service and food quality. You can contact us.
